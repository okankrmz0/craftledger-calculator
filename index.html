Quick answers

Which fees does this include?

Etsy's transaction fee on item + shipping, payment processing (percentage + fixed), the listing fee that renews on each sale, and — when toggled — the Offsite Ads fee. All rates are editable above.

Are the default rates right for my country?

Defaults reflect standard US rates. Payment processing varies by country, so open "Fee settings" and adjust to match your Etsy account, then bookmark the page.

Does this store or send my numbers anywhere?

No. Everything is calculated in your browser. Nothing is saved, sent, or tracked by this tool.
